Group buys protected by Solana escrow and explained by AI.

Snowball

Snowball lets student groups run bulk purchases with real Solana devnet escrow, AI risk explanations, and an honest proof page that separates live, fallback, demo-only, and roadmap features.

Problem

Student clubs and small groups coordinate expensive shared purchases in chats, spreadsheets, and bank transfers. One organizer or seller often holds everyone funds, which creates trust, refund, and delivery risk.

Solution

Buyers join a campaign, deposit into a Solana devnet escrow, and seller release happens only after the delivery confirmation threshold is met. AI explains each state in plain language for non-crypto users.

Judge Demo Path

  1. Open http://localhost:8082 and restart the demo.
  2. Connect Phantom or Solflare on Solana Devnet.
  3. Fund the wallet with local demo devnet SOL.
  4. Join the RTX 5080 campaign and sign the real devnet deposit.
  5. Open the receipt and verify the transaction on Explorer.
  6. Show AI Risk Report, Seller Dashboard, dispute/refund demo state, and Proof tab.

Live vs Demo

  • Fallback: LI.FI route preview and text AI when partner credentials are unavailable.
  • Demo-only: dispute and refund state, not on-chain instructions yet.
  • Roadmap: SPL USDC vaults and on-chain dispute/refund instructions.
